Zhan Qingyun

Born in Guizhou in 1990, Zhan Qingyun is a practicing lawyer, Chinese-language debater and writer.

She earned her bachelor’s degree in Economics from the Chinese University of Hong Kong and a Juris Doctor (J.D.) from Harvard University, holding a New York State attorney license in the United States. On her academic journey, she twice declined admission to Peking University and took out a million-dollar loan to pursue postgraduate studies at Harvard. Growing up as an ordinary girl from a small town, she has continuously expanded the boundaries of her life through knowledge.

A veteran of Chinese-language debating, she has won championships and Best Debater awards at the International Chinese Debate Invitational Tournament and the Chinese Debate World Cup. She rose to mainstream fame in Season 6 of I Can I BB, claiming the annual BBKing title, renowned for her rigorous logic, humanistic empathy and gentle yet sharp wit.

Currently an international commercial lawyer, she remains engaged with public issues, women’s development and critical thinking communication. She is the author of the autobiography I Insist on Being Stubborn While Young.

She measures reality with rationality, embraces the world with tenderness, and upholds her conscience. May we maintain independent thinking amid chaos, persist in speaking sincerely amid ordinariness, and grow into sober and resolute individuals through the power of critical reasoning.

詹青云，1990年生于贵州，执业律师、华语辩手、写作者。

本科就读于香港中文大学经济学系，哈佛大学法律博士（J.D.），持美国纽约州律师执业资格。求学路上，她两次拒绝北大，贷款百万赴哈佛深造，从普通小镇女孩，一步步以知识拓宽人生边界。

她深耕华语辩论多年，斩获国际华语辩论邀请赛、华语辩论世界杯冠军及最佳辩手；参加《奇葩说》第六季，夺得年度BBKing，以严密逻辑、人文共情与温柔锋芒被大众熟知。

现为国际商事律师，同时持续关注公共议题、女性成长与思辨表达，著有自传《趁着年轻，我偏要勉强》。

以理性丈量现实，以温柔拥抱世界，以良知守住本心。愿我们在喧嚣中保持独立思考，于平凡里坚持真诚发声，以思辨的力量，活成清醒而坚定的自己。
